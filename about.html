About LOGIQ Studio

Where human creativity meets artificial intelligence to build the future of marketing.

The Origin

Built for the AI Era

LOGIQ Studio was founded in 2025 with a simple belief: the best marketing happens when human creativity and AI efficiency work together, not in competition.

We saw agencies charging $10K+ for work that AI could accelerate dramatically — and we saw AI-only tools producing generic output that lacked strategic depth. So we built something different: a hybrid agency that leverages AI to generate at scale while relying on experienced human strategists to refine, optimize, and deliver results.

Today, we serve clients across 12+ industries and 8 countries, from bootstrapped startups to funded scaleups. Every project goes through our three-stage pipeline: AI generates, humans refine, data validates.
