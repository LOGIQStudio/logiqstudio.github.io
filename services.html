FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does your AI-human hybrid approach work?

We use AI tools (GPT-4, Gemini, Midjourney, RunwayML) to generate hundreds of creative variants, copy options, and strategic angles. Our human experts then hand-select and refine the best outputs, ensuring brand alignment, quality, and performance optimization before delivery.

What platforms do you support for ad campaigns?

We manage campaigns on Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads (Search/Display/YouTube), TikTok Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Reddit Ads, and X (Twitter) Ads. We choose the best platform mix based on your audience and goals.

How fast can you deliver?

Most packages deliver within 2–7 business days. Websites in 4–7 days. Ad audits in 2–3 days. Chatbots in 5–8 days. Retainer clients get priority turnaround with 24-hour response times.

Do you guarantee results?

We guarantee quality and transparency. For ad management, we set ROAS targets upfront and optimize continuously. If we don't hit agreed targets, we extend the optimization period at no extra cost. Our track record shows 4.8× average ROAS across all clients.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Wise, Payoneer, and PayPal for international clients. Invoices are sent via email. We require 50% upfront for project-based work and monthly billing for retainers.

Can I start with a single package and upgrade later?

Absolutely! Many clients start with an AI Ad Audit or content package and upgrade to a full retainer once they see results. There's no lock-in — you can mix and match packages as needed.
